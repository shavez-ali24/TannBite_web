We're Building the Restaurant OS for QR-First Dining

Tap N Bite started with a simple idea: ordering should be fast for guests, and fully controlled by restaurant owners—without needing a big IT team.

Why We Built Tap N Bite

Many restaurants today run on a mix of paper menus, messaging apps, and multiple disconnected tools. This makes it easy to lose orders, miscommunicate, or forget what was sold last night.

We designed Tap N Bite as a single, simple system where guests can place orders with a scan, and teams can manage everything from one clean dashboard.

Built for Real Service Hours

We designed the workflow to fit into busy lunch rushes and late-night service, with quick status changes and clear screens.

Simple, Not Overwhelming

Tap N Bite focuses on the essentials: ordering, menu management, sales visibility, and basic staff roles, without unnecessary complexity.

Future-Ready

With inventory and deeper analytics on the way, restaurants that start now will be ready for the next phase with no migration pain.

Our Product Principles

  • Fast to start – you don't need a designer or developer.
  • Easy to train – staff can learn the interface in minutes.
  • Owner control first – you decide what's visible and who can change what.
  • Real-world friendly – works on the devices you already own, in the browser.
