Pricing Questions

Will I have to pay later?

In the future we may add paid plans with extra features. If that happens, you'll be notified clearly and given time to decide what works best for you. We want Tap N Bite to stay accessible, especially for smaller businesses.

Are there any hidden fees?

No. During this free phase we do not charge for orders, tables, or staff accounts. Your own payment provider may charge transaction fees if you connect online payments in the future.

Can I export my data?

Yes. You will be able to export key data like sales reports so you're never locked in.
