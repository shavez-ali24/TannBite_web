Built for Modern, Fast-Moving Restaurants

Tap N Bite brings your entire ordering flow into one place: from QR code scan to completed order, with full control for restaurant owners.

Core Feature

QR Code Ordering at Every Table

Each table gets its own QR code. When a guest scans, we know exactly which table the order is coming from—no manual notes, no confusion, no shouting across the dining room.

  • Unique QR code for each table or pickup area
  • Guest sees live, up-to-date menu & availability
  • No need to install any app – opens in browser
  • Fewer mistakes and less back-and-forth with staff
Flexible Control

Flexible Order Types You Can Switch On and Off

Decide how you want to serve customers: dine-in, takeaway, delivery, or a combination. You are always in control of which order types are available.

  • Offer eat here, takeaway, and delivery from one screen
  • Turn each option on or off in seconds
  • Automatically show only the options you support
  • Reduce confusion for guests and staff
Real-Time Updates

Live Order Management with Clear Statuses

As soon as a guest places an order, it appears in your admin panel with a clear status. Your team can focus on preparation instead of chasing tickets.

  • Notification bell for every new incoming order
  • Simple status flow: pending → completed or cancelled
  • Track open orders by table or order number
  • Perfect for both front of house and kitchen teams
Full Control

Full Control of Your Digital Menu

Your menu is always under your control. Update items, change prices, or turn dishes off when they're out of stock—all from the admin panel.

  • Add, edit, and remove items with just a few clicks
  • Group dishes into categories like Starters, Mains, Desserts
  • Highlight specials or bestsellers
  • Instant updates across all guest devices
Analytics & Insights

Sales Analytics That Make Sense

Understand how your restaurant is performing with simple, visual reporting. No need to export spreadsheets or build your own dashboards.

  • Filter revenue by day, week, month, year, or all time
  • Spot patterns such as busiest days and top-selling items
  • Use charts to explain performance to partners
  • Export or screenshot reports to share with your team
Team Management

Staff Roles Designed for Busy Teams

Give your team access to the tools they need, and nothing more. Protect sensitive information while keeping operations smooth.

  • Create staff accounts for order handling
  • Staff can manage and update order statuses
  • They cannot change restaurant settings or menu design
  • Revenue and analytics stay visible only to admins
Kitchen Display

Kitchen Display System for Faster Prep

Send orders straight to the kitchen on a dedicated screen. Your back-of-house team sees what to cook, in what order, and when items are ready—without paper tickets or missed chits.

  • Large, readable tickets optimized for busy kitchens
  • Clear item breakdowns, modifiers, and special requests
  • Status updates so front-of-house knows when food is ready
  • Works alongside your existing QR and admin order flow
